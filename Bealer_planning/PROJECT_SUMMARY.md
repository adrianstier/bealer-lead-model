- Identify best bundling opportunities

### B. Automated Invoice & Envelope Mailing
**What it does:** Automatically sends paper invoices to older customers who need them

**Key Benefits:**
- Prevents missed payments
- Reduces senior customer frustration
- Improves retention in high-value segment
- Zero manual effort after setup

**Data Needed:**
- Monthly invoices (PDF)
- Customer addresses and ages
- Payment history
- Billing preferences

**Timeline:** Prototype Week 6, Live Week 7

### C. Cancellation Watchtower & Save System
**What it does:** Monitors cancellation risk and automates outreach

**Key Benefits:**
- Never miss a save opportunity
- Prioritizes highest-value at-risk customers
- Generates personalized save scripts
- Tracks premium at risk

**Data Needed:**
- Weekly cancel-pending reports
- Reason codes
- Customer communication history
- Premium amounts

**Timeline:** Dashboard ready Week 6, Automated outreach Week 7

**What it does:** Builds relationships through personalized automated communication

**Key Benefits:**

- Birthday and milestone messages
- Renewal summaries in plain English
- Local Santa Barbara content
- Post-claim check-ins

**Data Needed:**
- Customer demographics
- Birthdays and renewal dates
- Policy information
- Service history

**Timeline:** First newsletter Week 6, Fully automated Week 8

**Q: What if the AI makes a mistake?**
A: All communications reviewed before sending (at first). Over time, confidence thresholds ensure quality. You always have override capability.

**Q: How much of my data is needed?**
A: More data = better predictions. Minimum: 1 year. Ideal: 2-3 years. We start with what's available and improve over time.

**Q: Can I pause or stop this?**
A: Absolutely. Every system has an on/off switch. Phased rollout means you control pace.

**Q: What about Allstate compliance?**
A: All messaging follows Allstate guidelines. Legal review before any customer-facing content goes live.

**Q: What happens after 12 weeks?**

**Q: How is this different from buying leads?**
